Why Does Seal Have Face Scars? Details On His Illness And Facial Scars

The scar on Seal’s face is the result of a type of lupus called discoid lupus erythematosus.

The autoimmune disease attacks its own tissue and causes inflammation in the affected organ.

The condition specifically affects the skin above the neck. Some other parts could be the Joints, brain, lungs, kidneys, and blood vessels as well.

The type of scar occurs if not treated aggressively with sun protection and anti-inflammatory medicines.

Nevertheless, the prominent scar on his face is hard not to notice. 

Not only, Seal had received marks on their face, he also had to lose his hair.

Fortunately, the singer’s illness has been in remission for several years, albeit his scars remain forever. 

Seal Is Vocal About His Facial Scar & Disease

Singer Seal has remained vocal about his facial scars and disease. In an interview, he revealed about being diagnosed with this syndrome as a teen. 

Soon, at the age of 23 years, he developed scars as the result of lupus. Moreover, it also reached his scalp, eventually, he also went bald.
