Aggretsuko Season 5: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Trailer, and Other Important Details!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Aggretsuko, the hit animated series from Sanrio, has captivated audiences around the world with its relatable characters and relatable story of a young woman navigating the stresses of modern work life.

The series follows the titular Retsuko, a red panda who works as an accountant at a trading company in Tokyo. She deals with the daily frustrations of office politics and difficult coworkers. The fourth season premiered on the 16th of December 2021, and with the fifth and final season around the corner, here is everything we know about the upcoming show.

Aggretsuko Season 5 Release Date: When will it air?

Finally, the wait is paying off, as the show’s fifth season will premiere in February 2023. A specific date is yet to be set, but this will be released in due time, and the show will be available for streaming on Netflix.

Aggretsuko Season 5 Plot: What will happen?

Aggretsuko is centered on Retsuko’s struggles to find balance and joy in her life as she faces the constant pressure of working long hours and dealing with a demanding boss. But Retsuko finds a unique outlet for her frustrations through her secret hobby: death metal karaoke. Each night after work, Retsuko escapes to a karaoke bar and lets out her rage through the power of heavy metal music.

The fourth season ended with Haida quitting her job, Tadano becoming president of the space venture, and Himuro stepping down as the CEO. The fifth season plot remains unknown, but we are certain it will pick up from the season 4 ending.

Aggretsuko Season 5 Cast: Who will be returning?

The series’ main cast includes Kaolip as Retsuko, Shingo Kato as Haida Komegumi Koeda, Souta Arai as Director Ton, Rina Inoue as Tsinoda, Komegumi Koiwasaki as Ms. Wasimi . Currently, we do not know the season 5 cast lineup, but we are certain that the main cast will be back and reprising their roles.

Aggretsuko Review:

Reviews of the series have been overwhelmingly positive, with many praising the show’s relatable characters and relatable themes. IMDB has given the series an 8.5/10 rating. Similarly, Decider has also given a positive review, stating that the series is “a must-watch for anyone who has ever felt frustrated with their job.” As for whether to stream it or skip it, we highly recommend that you stream it!

Aggretsuko is a relatable, funny, and heartfelt series that will resonate with audiences of all ages. Aggretsuko is a must-watch series for anyone who has ever felt frustrated with their job. With relatable characters, relatable themes, and a killer soundtrack, this series will leave a lasting impression on viewers. So be sure to tune in for the fifth season of Aggretsuko and watch Retsuko continue navigating modern work life’s pressures with her unique brand of death metal karaoke.

Aggretsuko Season 5 Trailer: When will it air?

A trailer for the fifth season of the Aggretsuko series has already been released, and you can check it out on either Netflix or YouTube.
