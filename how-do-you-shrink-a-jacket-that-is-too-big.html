  • Turn the jacket inside out.
  • Gather the fabric along the side seams of the jacket until it is pulled to your desired size.

  • Cut away excess fabric along the new seam to complete the alteration.

  • Pin the top of the shoulder to the desired shape.
  • Sew along the line of pins, removing them as you sew.
  • Remove any excess fabric.
  • Turn the coat right-side out.
  • Press the shoulder while the coat is right-side out.

    Is it OK to vacuum seal a down jacket?

    Fluffy items such as coats, insulated jackets and sleeping bags that have plenty of air trapped in them to operate optimally should never be vacuum sealed. When you suck the air out of these items, their fabric is compressed and stays in that state for a long time. There is no great way to fluff them up again.
