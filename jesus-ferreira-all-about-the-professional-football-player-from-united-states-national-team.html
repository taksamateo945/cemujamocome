Jesus Ferreira- All About The Professional Football Player From United States National Team

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 5, 2024

Jesus Ferreira works as a professional football player. He has been with the USL Championship club Tulsa Roughnecks since 2018, was loaned out by Major League club FC Dallas, and has represented the US national team since 2020. He plays forward for both the club and the national team.

Where was Jesus Ferreira born?

On December 24, 2000, in Santa Marta, Colombia, Jesus was born to his father, David Ferreira, and mother, Mandy Ferreira. Jesus’ father was a professional football player who made his debut for the club FC Dallas, among others. Jesus was the Ferreira family’s first child. He has two older brothers. His older brother’s name is Santiago Ferreira, and his younger brother’s name is Matthew Ferreira.

How did Jesus get acquainted with football?

Jesus was exposed to football by his father. Jesus used to watch his father’s matches on his computer since his father was a professional football player. He was unable to see his father’s live games since David used to play for Colombian-affiliated teams.

Jesus’ Secondary Goal

Jesus aspired to be a zoologist, but his professional existence as a football player prevented him from achieving his goal. Despite the fact that he couldn’t be a zoologist, he created the farm with the money he earned as a football player. His property is home to a wide range of animals.

He is ready to help the national team at any moment.

In an interview, Jesus said that it feels amazing when he receives a call from a national camp. According to Jesus, performing for the national team is where he can show off his club-level abilities. Jesus said that playing with clubs has improved his performance each season and that the national team would be an excellent platform for him to demonstrate his abilities.

Friendship and football should be balanced for Jesus’ sake.

Jesus had a close friendship with his professional football player pal Ricardo Pepi. Jesus said that they began their football careers together by enrolling at the same academy. They even started their professional careers at the same club, thus they had a strong relationship. However, Jesus and Pepi presently play for separate clubs but for the same national team, thus he said that competing with his own buddy would be the happiest experience.

Jesus said that he wanted to see the complete over Pepi’s abilities and to exhibit his best against him. Jesus said that his friendship with Pepi was in excellent standing, therefore he was overjoyed to see him at the national camps.

Will Jesus appear at the FIFA World Cup in Qatar in 2022?

Jesus is now making his debut with the United States national team, which qualified for the FIFA World Cup 2022 by finishing second in CONCACAF’s third round. It is Jesus’ first World Cup. As a forward player, he will represent his country.

Net Worth

Jesus’ net worth is believed to be $1.5 million as of August 2023. As an FC Dallas player, he earns $200K every season.
