Who Are Myles Rowser Parents? Who Is Campbell University Football Player? Wikipedia and Age

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 12, 2024

Myles Rowser is a footballer from Campbell University. Reportedly, he signed for the FCS Campbell on February 2, 2022.

Earlier, he attended Belleville High School. Most probably, he had started his football career there.

According to Times Record, he is the no. 4 player in Michigan. Also, he is marked as the no. 20 safety in his class.

Huge speculations were claiming that Myles could end up at Michigan again. However, the tables turned as the prospect has found his new team.

Undoubtedly, he would have been a great signing for Arkansas’ 2022 class. Reportedly, he is experienced at running back and multiple spots on defense.

The National Recruiting Analyst Allen Trieu claims that he is a varsity player who has given great competitions in practice as well as in games.

Indeed, he takes good angles to the ball and is a very reliable tackler. Nevertheless, he is yet to improve explosiveness and top end.

Originally, he is from Detroit, Michigan. Thus, we can confirm his nationality as American. But, he is yet to speak regarding his ethnicity and family background.
