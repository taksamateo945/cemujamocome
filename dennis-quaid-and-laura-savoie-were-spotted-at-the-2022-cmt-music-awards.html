Dennis Quaid And Laura Savoie Were Spotted At The 2022 CMT Music Awards

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 12, 2024

Dennis Quaid and his wife Laura Savoie were seen on the red carpet of the 2022 CMT Music Awards on April 11 in Nashville, Tennessee. At their first red carpet event in three years, the couple showed off their love and stylish elegance. Quaid was dressed in a black t-shirt, thin trousers, a silver jacket, and metallic boots, while Savoie was dressed in a black jumpsuit and tiny silver jewelry.

Everything there is to know about Dennis Quaid’s wife

Laura Savoie, born February 21, 1993, is a Ph.D. student at the University of Texas. She is well known as the wife of Oscar winner Dennis Quaid. Savoie attended Pepperdine University in California and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting. In 2015, she earned a Master’s degree in Accounting from the University of Notre Dame in Indiana.

Savoie was the Accountancy Association’s vice president and the Class Legacy Leader. She also studied for an MBA in Sao Paolo, Brazil, and Santiago, Chile. Savoie is now earning her Ph.D. at the University of Texas at Austin’s Red McCombs School of Business, where she also works as a teaching assistant. Following her Master’s degree, she worked at Ernst & Young for two years, where she was awarded the Ernst & Young Outstanding Student Scholarship. She also has accounting, real estate, and yoga certificates, and has worked with a variety of organizations in California and Mexico. Her net worth is reported to be in the $300,000 range.

The history of Dennis Quaid and Laura Savoie’s relationship

Dennis Quaid and Laura Savoie were apparently dating for many months before disclosing their romance to the world on May 14, 2019, at Craig’s in West Hollywood. The couple snapped many photos at the celebrity hotspot in California and published a handful of them on social media. Quaid proposed to Savoie in October 2019, and the couple married in Santa Barbara in June 2020.

The wedding was previously slated for April 2020 in Hawaii, along with a celebration for friends and family. However, because to the COVID-19 epidemic, the plans had to be altered. For their secret wedding, the couple eloped to Santa Barbara. They exchanged conventional vows and Bulgari rings in the presence of a preacher. Savoie held a bouquet of roses and calla lilies and wore a gown by Chosen By One Day.

Despite the fact that Savoie is 40 years younger than Dennis, the age gap has never worried them. Quaid recently stated:

“I didn’t go out hunting for an age difference or someone much younger than me.” I met her at a work function, and our friendship grew from there. You have no say in who you fall in love with. I don’t easily fall in love. But I can’t allow what a few people believe dictate everything. I’ve been married three times, and I know this is the last one. “I feel like I have a true life companion.”
