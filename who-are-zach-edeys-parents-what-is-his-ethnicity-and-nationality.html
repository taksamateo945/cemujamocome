Zach Edeys Parents, His Ethnicity and Nationality

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Zach Edey’s parents are Julia and Glen Edey, who are believed to be Chinese immigrants to Canada. This has made Zach, who was born in Toronto, a Chinese-Canadian national and of Asian ethnicity.

Julia and Glen have refused to open up about their personal lives and career endeavors, but their son is a known collegiate basketball player in the Big Ten Conference. He currently plays with the team Purdue Boilermakers.

A Look  At Zach Edey’s Parents and Family Background

From what is perceivable, Zach Edey falls under the category of celebs that are averse to bringing the details of their early life and family background to the public arena. However, fans and the general public keep getting tidbits from time to time.

It came to the fore that the Big Ten Conference player’s parents are Glen Edey (father) and his wife, Julia Edey. They welcomed Zach Edey on the 14th of May 2002, and the basketballer’s place of birth is captured by the records as Toronto, Ontario in Canada, where he also spent a better part of his growing up years.

As earlier mentioned, the detailed history of his lineage has remained under the shade since Zach burst onto the limelight. However, the fillers we are getting say the Edey family are most likely Chinese immigrants who decided to put down roots in Canada.

Zach is not the only child of his parents; he was brought up alongside a younger brother named Dough Edey. Dough is also athletic like his big brother, and he has been representing his school Leaside High School in athletics.

Since Julia is no celebrity, it is difficult to get information about her early life and family background. What is Zach Edey’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

Considering his birth details above, Zach Edey is of Canadian nationality and is from Asian ethnic background. Internet sources listed the giant player as a man of Chinese-Canadian roots, which rings true since his parents have been revealed to have come from the Asian continent.

With that said, we must also note that his great height of 7 feet 4 inches is somewhat uncommon in the Asian continent, especially in China, where their men don’t grow past the average height of 5 feet 7.6 inches, which is the equivalent of 171.8 cm.
