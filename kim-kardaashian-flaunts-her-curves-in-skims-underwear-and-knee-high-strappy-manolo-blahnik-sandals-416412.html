Kim Kardaashian Flaunts Her Curves In Skims Underwear And Knee-High Strappy Manolo Blahnik Sandals

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 24, 2024

Kim Kardashian is serving some wild looks on Instagram as she is showing off her Skims body bra and body thong, but it is her footwear that is going viral. Kim shared a  series of sultry shots where she flaunted her curves while wearing a pair of Manolo Blahnik sandals. Kim took the straps and instead of keeping them low, and bunched up around her ankles, stretched them out to her knees. The look drew plenty of attention and photos of Kim wearing the sexy footwear are going viral. Kim shared photos on her official Instagram account, where she has 179 million followers. She also posted several pictures of herself wearing the Manolo Blahnik shoes with a variety of outfits on the Skims Instagram page that has an additional 1.8 million followers.

In the photo below, Kim wore the Pricestro 105 heels sandals from Manolo Blahnik as she posed on a sofa, while seated in a limousine. The shoes accentuated her legs, making them appear longer than her 5’3″ frame. Kim rested her hand against the top of her head and showed off her newly-dyed red hair as she looked directly at the camera.
