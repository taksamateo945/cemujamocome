Game of Thrones' the Red Viper, the Mountain Bond in Shirtless Pic!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, January 26, 2024

Spoiler Alert! Don't read any further if you haven't yet watched the Sunday, June 1 episode of Game of Thrones.

Game of Thrones may have delivered yet another gruesome, horrifying death in the HBO saga's latest episode—but real-life stars Pedro Pascal (Prince Oberyn Martell, a.k.a. the Red Viper) and Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson (Gregor Clegane, a.k.a. The Mountain) aren't bearing grudges.

Pascal shared a bare-chested picture via Instagram on Monday, June 2, showing the stunning size difference between him and the man who crushed his character to death, as they flexed their muscles in the sunshine.

The pair shared the stage in one of the most gripping fight scenes ever on the hit series over the weekend. The Red Viper faced The Mountain in what was both a trial-by-battle and a settling of old scores: Clegane had raped and murdered Oberyn's sister in the past.
