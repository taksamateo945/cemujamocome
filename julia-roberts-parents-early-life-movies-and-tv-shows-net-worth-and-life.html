Julia Roberts Parents, Early Life, Movies and Tv Shows, Net Worth and Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 18, 2024

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Growing up in Smyrna, Georgia, Julia Roberts shared her childhood with two older siblings, Eric and Lisa.Her educational journey included attending Fitzhugh Lee Elementary School, Griffin Middle School, and Campbell High School.

Following high school graduation, she relocated to New York City, driven by her ambition to pursue an acting career.

Her breakout role came in the 1989 film Mystic Pizza, setting the stage for a series of successful films such as Pretty Woman (1990), Erin Brockovich (2000), and Ocean’s Eleven (2001).

Recognized for her outstanding performances, Roberts earned accolades, including an Academy Award for Best Actress for Erin Brockovich.

Production and other roles

Beyond acting, Roberts made a mark as a successful producer, overseeing films like Mona Lisa Smile (2003), Charlie Wilson’s War (2007), and Eat Pray Love (2010) through her production company, Red Om Films, which she co-founded.

Julia Roberts extends her influence beyond the entertainment world.

As a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador, she actively participates in numerous charitable organizations, demonstrating a commitment to philanthropy.

Additionally, Roberts is a vocal advocate for animal rights, using her platform to champion important causes.

What is her net worth?

Julia Roberts boasts an estimated net worth of $250 million, accumulated through her endeavors in film and television, along with endorsements and various business ventures.

As one of the most successful actresses in history, Roberts commands high salaries for her work, contributing significantly to her wealth.

Her fame has also translated into lucrative endorsement deals with renowned brands like Givenchy, Lancôme, and L’Oréal.

Julia Roberts Parents, Husband and Kids

Since 2002, Julia Roberts has been married to Danny Moder, a cinematographer. Together, they share three children: twins Hazel and Phinnaeus (born 2004) and son Henry (born 2007).

While this is Roberts’ current and enduring marriage, she has been married twice before. Her first marriage, in 1993, was to country singer Lyle Lovett, but the couple divorced just two years later.

In 1998, Roberts was engaged to actor Benjamin Bratt, yet they called off their engagement in 2001.

Speaking about her present marriage to Moder, Roberts expresses great happiness. She acknowledges him for contributing to the balance she has found in both her life and career.
