Quick facts

Early life and Childhood

Tamar Braxton’s full name is Tamar Estine Braxton, born on March 17th, 1977. Her mother is Evelyn Braxton, and Michael Conrad Braxton is her father. She was born in Maryland, United States, and is the youngest member of her family.

Tamar has four sisters and a brother. The four sisters are Traci Braxton, Toni Braxton, and Trina Braxton.

Her only brother is Michael Conrad Braxton Jr. Tamar Braxton is the youngest member of her family band, making her name famous.

She lived her early life with her parents and siblings. Tamar has held a strong connection with her family until now.

Tamar is a beautiful woman with a stunning personality. She attracts the attention of millions of her followers.

She has dark hair and dark eyes.

Relationships and Dating History

Braxton’s married life has been through thick and thin. Settling for the love of her life has been quite problematic as love is complicated these days.

Who is her first husband?

Braxton married Darrell Allamby in 2001. Allamby was a producer and songwriter who made several songs. The couple met while working together on the debut track in 2000.

The marriage didn’t last very long as the rumors of their separation started surfacing. The couple separated after two years of their marriage.

Tamar Braxton and Vincent Herbert

Tamar and Vincent started dating each other in 2003 through Toni, her sister. They finally got married on November 27th, 2008.

She and Vincent even had their first child, Logan Vincent Herbert, in 2003. Despite their commitment, the couple separated in 2017. The primary reason for the divorce was irreconcilable differences.

Who is Tamar Braxton’s boyfriend?

Tamar was in a relationship with David Adefeso. He is a financial advisor and a renowned businessman.

Sources say the couple met at a birthday party in 2018. David completed his Master in Business Administration (MBA) from Harvard University. He achieved massive success by working at Walmart and various other sectors.

People knew David as a multi-millionaire from the age of 32. He is active in several businesses now.

After Tamar attempted suicide, David filed a domestic violence case against her.

Career

For Tamar, singing has always been her interest and passion. She has been singing since she was a child. Braxton used to sing in the church with her father.

She entered the music industry with her first album at Arista Records. Tamar and her sisters launched their first single. Their first single was ‘Good Life,’ which was unsuccessful initially.

The age difference between the sisters made it challenging to promote the song. Toni Braxton got separated from the group at first. So for Toni, Tamar and her sisters sang as backups.

Tamar, Towanda, and Trina are the only ones left in the group. They released their album, ‘So Many Ways,’ which became very popular.

Tamar started her solo career in 2000. Her solo songs didn’t get that much attention from the public.

While she was busy making her solo albums, she still worked by her sister, Toni Braxton’s side. Then, Tamar’s solo albums slowly started getting recognition.

Braxton also worked as a model and co-host while getting recognition as a solo artist. Tamar Braxton makes history with her win on ‘Celebrity Big Brother’ as the first Black person to win the show. 

She also acted in the movie ‘True To The Game,’ which premiered on April 10th, 2020. She also released the song, ‘Crazy Kind of Love.’
