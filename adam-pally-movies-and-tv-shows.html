Adam Pally’s Early Life and Education

Adam Pally, born on March 18, 1982, hails from a diverse background, having grown up in New York City, Chicago, and New Jersey. Coming from a Jewish family, Pally’s upbringing was influenced by his cultural heritage. He attended The New School University in New York, graduating in 2004 with a degree in Fine Arts.

During his time at university, Pally honed his comedic skills at the renowned Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre. This improvisational and sketch comedy training would prove to be instrumental in his future success as an actor and comedian.

With a natural talent for making people laugh, Pally quickly established himself in the comedy scene, joining renowned groups such as “Death by Roo Roo” and “ASSSSCAT 3000.” His dedication to the craft and passion for comedy set the stage for his subsequent Hollywood career.
